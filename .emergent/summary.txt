<product_requirements>
The goal is to develop a minimalist mobile app for international students to connect before arriving at their new institution. Users create profiles (origin/destination, university, course) and can search/filter others. Key features include following, connection requests, and real-time chat (after acceptance). Users can create public posts (text, images, videos, GIFs) visible if at least one filter matches; followers can interact (like, comment, share via DM). Verification is critical: only .edu email, student ID, and live photo verification enable posting; others can view and chat. Main screens: Profile, Settings (deep-nested delete), Posts (landing page with creation), and Events (create/join, discussion). Special features include an AI helper for queries and post discovery.
</product_requirements>
